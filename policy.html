Rules & Policies

Rules

Like many other servers on the platform, rules are set in order to keep the community and its members safe. Though regular moderation is in place to guide people onto the right path, moderators should not be viewed as a means to restrict you from chatting. They are only there to act as a means of enforcement of the rules and will only take action when someone is endangering another member or the community. With that being said, here are the rules in this server:

Rule 1

No doxxing or harassment (especially threats on someone’s life/property) or encouraging self harm. See the Doxing Policy Explainer for more information.

Rule 2

No spamming, phishing, or attempting to steal another user’s account (broadly speaking, one could consider this “no spamming or scamming”).

Rule 3

No sharing pirated content.

Rule 4

Content that is explicitly pornographic, depicting sexual acts, or depicting nudity is prohibited. There will be Zero Tolerance

Rule 5

No child porn, revenge porn or gore/animal cruelty anywhere in the server. See the Teen and Child Safety Policy Explainer for more information. There will be Zero Tolerance

Rule 6

Discriminatory jokes and language related to one’s race, age, gender, disability, etc. are prohibited.

Rule 7

Do not evade enforcement actions by any of our staff (e.g. Creating another account to bypass a ban)

Rule 8

Most Importantly, Oblige by Discord's Community Guidelines. We will be using that document as a base for moderation, so please follow any and all rules listed in the guidelines as well as the rules here. We will be somewhat lenient on the community guidelines.

General Discord Rules

You must be 13 years or older to use Discord.

Distributing “auto,” “macro,” or “cheat utility” programs as well as providing hacked/modded game software through Discord is prohibited.

General Server Policy

Since the server is meant for gathering communities together, we advise all members to have an open mind and encourage members from other communities and/or hobbies to join them. We want everyone to feel included and have the ability to explore new possible hobbies.

Moderator Policy

Similar to normal members, our moderators will serve using a different policy. This policy is meant to guide the moderator and doesn't apply to normal members. You are still free to follow these policies as a member, but please keep in mind that this moderator policy will not apply to you.
